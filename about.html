about

We are a team of Wellesley students dedicated to bettering our community. We wanted to change the way that our community comes together by making it easier to find each other. Our website lets you advertise for buddies so that you can collaborate with people you may not find otherwise. It's a way for people to learn with their peers and to make friends while they do it. Our website will also created network connections for the future. Networking is an integral part of Wellesley siblinghood and strengthening that is exciting for us. We've always wanted an easier way to find study buddies and now we have it!
